Frequently asked.

Does the license work for more than one Mac? How many can I use the license on?

One Grid license can be used for up to 3 activations.

Why is Grid not on the App Store?

Apple requires sandboxing for new apps in the App Store. Grid needs to move and resize windows and that prevents it from being sandboxed.

I feel more secure paying on the Apple App Store.

For payment, we use a trusted payment partner and secure payment methods (PayPal & Apple Pay).
